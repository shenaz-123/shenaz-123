Chicken Biryani Recipe
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 1Kg
Chicken 1Kg
water 2litre
onion 2 big size
green chillies 4-5 pieces
salt 20gm
oil 4 tbs
chilli powdwer 2 tbs
turmeric powder 2 tsp
garam masala powder 1 tsp
chicken Biryani Masala 1 tbs
ginger garlic paste 2 tsp
lemon 1 pcs
cardamom 8 pcs
cloves 10 pcs
Bay leaves 2 pcs
cinnamon 2 pcs
jeera 1 tsp
Yogurt 250 gms
Coriander 1 bunch
Mint 1 bunch

Preparation

Step 1 : Choose any chicken parts to marinate.

Step 2 : Wash the chicken and pat it dry.

Step 3 : Then add Chilli powder, turmaric powder, garam masala powder and biryani masala.

Step 4: Mix the contents well. Make sure the chicken is fully covered with the marinate.

Step 5: Add yogurt and 1/2 a lemon to the marinated chicken and mix well.

Step 6: Add 2tbs of oil to the marinated chicken and Keep in the fridge for atleast 6-8 hours.

Step 7: Meanwhile add 1 kg of basmati rice to a large pot and rinse it at least thrice. Drain and soak in fresh water for 30 mins. Drain to a colander after 30 mins

Cooking chicken

Step 1: Heat a oil in a heavy bottom pot . Make sure you use a large one for this and add cloves,cardamom,cinnamon,Bay leaves and jeera in to the pan.

Step 2: Add thinly sliced onions and green chillies. On a medium heat, fry them stirring often until uniformly light brown. Do not burn them as they leave a bitter taste

Step 3: Add marinated chicken & saute until it becomes pale for 5 minutes .Lower the flame completely. Cover and cook until the chicken is soft, tender and completely cooked.

Step 4: Check if the chicken is cooked by pricking with a fork or knife. It has to be just cooked and not overdone. Evaporate any excess moisture left in the pot by cooking further without the lid.

Step 5: Taste test and add more salt if needed. I added Â¼ more salt here.

Making chicken Biryani

  • Heat a heavy bottomed pot and add the chicken to it. Cook for 4 minutes and then turn the chicken pieces once. Cover and cook for another 3 minutes. Turn off the heat.

  • Scatter half the onions all over the chicken, and then sprinkle all the coriander and mint leaves. Next layer the rice all over the mint and coriander, and oil all over the rice. Cover and cook on a low flame for 20 minutes.

  • Once the biryani is cooked, let it rest for 5-10 minutes, and finish by scattering the remaining onions on top.

  • Serve hot digging the spoon deep to get all the layers.
