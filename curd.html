Cucumber Raita
Ingredients Quantity
Yogurt 1 cup
Medium onion 1 size
cucumber 1/2 cup
salt 1/4 tsp
green chillies 4-5 pieces
jeera powder 1 tsp
coriander 1 bunch

Preparation

Step 1: Chill the Yogurt.Rinse and chopped the onion and green chiillies.Rinse and peel the cucumber and chop to bite sizes.

Step 2: Rinse coriander leaves in ample amount of water.drain completely and chop them fine.

Step 3: Roast 1tsp cumin seeds on a lo flame until it mlls good.Cool this and crush them to fine powder in a spice jar.

Making Raita

  • Add yogurt and salt to a mixing bowl.Whisk them until smooth with a fork or a whisk.

  • Next add chopped cucumbers,onions,cumin powder,coriander leaves and green chillies.

  • Stir gently.If required add more yogurt.Taste test and add more salt if required.

  • Garnish with cumin powder on top.Serve raita with chicken biryani.
